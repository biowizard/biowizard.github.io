--- layout: mdpage --- ## Analysis as simple as
$ biowizard map-Day0-and-Day2-timepoints.yml Downloading Files 100% 0:00 remaining Running Commands 100% 0:00 remaining Uploading Results 76% 5:23 remaining
A lot of bioinformatics software is powerful and freely available, but has * Complicated lists of parameters and arguments * Documentation varying in quality from good to terrible to nonexistent * a tendency to fail hours into a running job Biowizard is built on a set of frequently used informatics tools including 1. samtools 1. bedtools 1. Cufflinks 1. STAR 1. MISO Mostly, Biowizard is here to save you sanity and time. A week saved from banging your head against a tool is one week closer to publication.
